What's New

  • Yarning Circle

    Yarning is an informal conversation that is culturally friendly and recognised by Aboriginal people as meaning to talk about something, someone or provide and receive information. Yarning Circles are designed to allow all students to have their say in a safe space without judgement.This was made for students to develop a deeper understanding about Aboriginal peoples culture.

  • Phone Ban

    We have put a ban on phones,so students stay focused on lessons instead of wasting their time.Their phones will be kept in the office until, the end of the day when students can pick them up.
